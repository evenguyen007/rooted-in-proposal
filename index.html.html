The Diagnosis

Three things the audit made impossible to ignore.

01

Your ceiling is real. Your floor has dropped.

In January 2026, one post hit 285,000 views and 22,423 likes. Another hit 59,000 views. The ceiling isn't theoretical - you reached it twice in one month. But since February, most posts are landing between 300 and 700 views. Not a creative problem. A pattern that stopped working.

Top post · 285K views · 22.4K likes Mar–Apr avg · 400–700 views

Opportunity: reverse-engineer the hook structure of your three viral posts. Produce 4–6 Reels per month that match that structure exactly.

02

The carousel is the lever you haven't pulled.

Reel ER: 0.80%. Carousel ER: 0.06%. Thirteen times lower. But carousels are where saves happen - the strongest long-tail signal Instagram's algorithm rewards in 2026. You've published 8 carousels in 100 posts, none structured as true guides. The wellness creators building Explore reach this year are almost all winning with save-first carousels.

Reel ER 0.80% · Carousel ER 0.06% 8 of 100 posts · 0 guide-format

Opportunity: 2 guide-style carousels per month - e.g. "Magnesium types: which one actually does what." Target 3–5x save increase within 90 days.

03

Your comment funnel is loud, and your audience has learned the song.

56 of 100 posts end with "Comment [WORD] and I'll send you." Brilliant funnel - when it's fresh. But when 89% of Reels use the same pattern, the audience recognizes the move and responds less over time. Your Mar–Apr data shows exactly that fatigue. The fix isn't dropping the CTA. It's alternating it with 2–3 no-CTA, story-only posts per month to reset trust before you ask.

56 of 100 posts with comment trigger Overall ER 0.71% vs 1–3% benchmark

Opportunity: a CTA rotation plan plus monthly story-only posts. Restore baseline engagement before layering back the funnel.
